reconnect – realign – reimagine – rediscover


Our vision is to offer a safe place, where people can come to relax, to re-connect with themselves and nature and realign themselves with their path in life.


It is all too easy to get caught up in a busy, to-do list society and then suffer burn out. Here at Red Roots Farm, we value slowing down, quietening our monkey minds, and coming to our senses.

We mind ourselves, each other and the land, and that is our core mission here at Red Roots Farm.


Red Roots Farm got its name from just this, a practice of leaving your thoughts and coming back to the land. In the 7 main chakra points in our body, our base chakra located at the bottom of our spine is also called our root chakra and its colour is red. During meditation we would imagine big strong red roots stretch deep into the earth, connecting and supporting us to grow into our full potential. Once we said ‘Red Roots Farm’ out loud, it was a keeper!


Tom Leavy, horticulturist & Claire Bracken artist have drawn on their skills and their passions of sustainability, wellness and creativity to create this peaceful haven on their family homestead in the heart centre of Ireland.
