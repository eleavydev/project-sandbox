Welcome to our Sustainability and Wellness Centre: Red Roots Farm! We are based in the heart of Ireland just oustide the small village of Killucan in Co. Westmeath. Our vision is to offer a safe and peaceful haven, where people can come to relax, let go, reconnect with themselves, resdiscover nature and realign themselves with their best path in life.



We know how easy it to get caught up in today's modern, busy world and feel mind and body burnout. Here at Red Roots Farm, we value slowing down, quietening our minds, feeling our feelings and coming to our senses. We mind ourselves, each other and the land, and that is our core mission.


Red Roots Farm got its name from just this, a practice of leaving your thoughts and coming back to the land.

In the 7 main chakra points in our body, our base chakra located at the bottom of our spine is also called our root chakra and its colour is red. During meditation we would imagine big strong red roots stretch deep into the earth, connecting and supporting us to grow into our full potential. Once we said ‘Red Roots Farm’ out loud, it was a keeper!


Who we are


We are Tom Leavy (Horticulturist) & Claire Bracken (Artist) and together we have drawn on our skills and our passions of sustainability, wellness and creativity to create this peaceful haven on our family homestead in the heart centre of Ireland.
